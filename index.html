About Me

Nick is a musician, music teacher, and cook with experience in various arenas of the music and food industries. He has worked on composition, recording, and mixing of several North Carolina musical artists including Hello Hugo, Giant Giants Improv Ensemble, The River Otters, Ouroboros Boys, Pepper Pulp (Ween cover band) and many others. He has performed solo and with different bands opening for nationally and internationally touring acts such Zammuto (featuring Nick Zammuto of The Books), Man Or Astroman, Perhaps, Marco Benevento Trio, Ahleuchatistas,Tashi Dorji, and many more, as well as performing original feature music for theater and arial dance productions. His composition style usually includes elements of ambient, noise, jazz, post-rock, and math-rock.
